The Margin Armor Protocol™

01

The Bid Autopsy

We don't guess. We pull your last 5 completed projects. We line up the "Estimate" vs. the "Actuals" line-by-line. We find the exact drift percentage. Usually, it's 8-12% lost on unbilled change orders and material creep. We fix this number instantly.

02

The Cashflow Cofferdam

We install a banking hierarchy. Operating expenses never touch profit. Tax money never touches payroll. We set up physical bank accounts that act as dams. When a deposit hits, it’s automatically split. You will never panic at tax time again.

03

The 5-Minute Dashboard

I don't give you complex spreadsheets. I build you a mobile-friendly dashboard. Every morning at 7:00 AM, you see 5 numbers: Cash on Hand, burn rate, accounts receivable, accounts payable, and True Net Profit. You run your business from your truck, not your desk.
